## Legal Sources for Classical Music

### Free & Public Domain
- **Musopen.org** - Royalty-free classical recordings
- **IMSLP** - International Music Score Library Project
- **Internet Archive** - Classical music collection
- **FreePD.com** - Public domain recordings

### Commercial Licensing
- **AudioJungle** - Royalty-free music marketplace
- **Epidemic Sound** - Subscription-based music licensing
- **Classical music labels** - Direct licensing

## File Requirements

- **Format**: MP3 (recommended) or WAV
- **Quality**: 128kbps minimum, 320kbps preferred
- **Length**: Full pieces or edited for bedtime (3-6 minutes ideal)
- **Volume**: Normalized to consistent levels

## Copyright Notice

Ensure all audio files are properly licensed for your intended use. While the compositions may be public domain, recordings are often still under copyright protection.
